How to Play Battleship

  • Each player places their fleet of ships on a hidden grid
  • Take turns calling out coordinates to attack opponent's grid
  • Mark hits and misses to track your progress
  • Sink all of your opponent's ships to win the game
  • Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2)
